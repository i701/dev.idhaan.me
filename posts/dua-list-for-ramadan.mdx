> May allah forgive all our sins. Happy ramadan! Stay blessed everyone!

#### Dua while breaking fast 🥛

<Dua
  dua="ذهب الظمأ وابتلت العروق وثبت الأجر إن شاء الله"
  meaning="Gone is the thirst. Moist are the veins. And, God willing, established is the reward."
/>

#### Dua for good in this world & next 🌎

<Dua
  dua="رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ"
  meaning="Our Lord! Give us good in the world and good in the Hereafter, and save us from the torment of the Fire of Hell (Surat Al-Baqarah, 2:201)."
/>

#### Dua for fogiveness 👐🏽

<Dua
  dua="رَبِّ اجْعَلْنِي مُقِيمَ الصَّلاَةِ وَمِن ذُرِّيَّتِي رَبَّنَا وَتَقَبَّلْ دُعَاءِ رَبَّنَا اغْفِرْ لِي وَلِوَالِدَيَّ وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ يَوْمَ يَقُومُ الْحِسَابُ"
  meaning="My Lord, make me an establisher of prayer, and from my descendants. Our Lord, and accept my supplication. Our Lord, forgive me and my parents and the believers the Day the account is established. [Quran; 14:40-41]."
/>

#### Dua for repentance 👐🏽

<Dua
  dua="اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي ذَنْبِي كُلَّهُ دِقَّهُ وَجِلَّهُ وَأَوَّلَهُ وَآخِرَهُ وَعَلاَنِيَتَهُ وَسِرَّهُ"
  meaning="O Lord, forgive me all my sins, small and great, first and last, open and secret. [Hadith; Muslim 483]"
/>

#### Dua for jannah ✨

<Dua
  dua="رَبِّ ابْنِ لِي عِندَكَ بَيْتًا فِي الْجَنَّة"
  meaning="My Lord, build for me near You a house in Paradise… [Quran; 66:11]"
/>

#### Dua for mindfullness

<Dua
  dua="رَبِّ أَعِنِّي عَلَى ذِكْرِكَ وَشُكْرِكَ وَحُسْنِ عِبَادَتِكَ"
  meaning="My Lord, help me to remember You, give thanks to You and worship You well. [Hadith; Nasa’i 1303]"
/>
